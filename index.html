Our Mission & Vision

Who We Are

GlobalHope International is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating lasting change in the lives of children, families, and communities living in poverty and injustice around the world.

Our Mission

To serve the world's most vulnerable people by alleviating poverty, providing emergency relief, and supporting sustainable development projects that empower communities to build better futures.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to basic necessities, quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunitiesâ€”a world where no child goes to bed hungry and every community can thrive.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach our work with empathy and understanding.
  • Integrity: We are transparent and accountable in all we do.
  • Collaboration: We work with communities, not just for them.
  • Innovation: We seek creative solutions to complex problems.
  • Sustainability: We focus on long-term impact rather than short-term fixes.

About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2005, GlobalHope International began as a small group of volunteers responding to the devastating tsunami in Southeast Asia. What started as emergency relief efforts quickly evolved into a comprehensive approach to addressing poverty and injustice around the world.

Our Approach

We believe in sustainable solutions that address the root causes of poverty. Our approach focuses on:

  • Community-led development
  • Partnerships with local organizations
  • Long-term commitment to the regions we serve
  • Measuring and evaluating impact

Where We Work

We currently operate in over 30 countries across Africa, Asia, Latin America, and the Middle East, focusing on regions where needs are greatest and resources are most limited.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals from diverse backgrounds including international development, public health, education, and business. 90% of our staff are local to the regions we serve, ensuring cultural understanding and community trust.

Our Projects

We implement a wide range of projects focused on sustainable development, emergency response, and community empowerment.

Financial Disclosure

Financial Overview

GlobalHope International is committed to using donations effectively and efficiently. We maintain the highest standards of financial accountability and transparency.

2023 Financial Snapshot

  • Total Revenue: $8,542,150
  • Program Expenses: $7,215,830 (85% of total expenses)
  • Administrative Expenses: $765,200 (9% of total expenses)
  • Fundraising Expenses: $561,120 (6% of total expenses)

We are proud to have earned a 4-star rating from Charity Navigator for 8 consecutive years, recognizing our commitment to financial health, accountability, and transparency.
